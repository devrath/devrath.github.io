01 My contributions

As Senior Software Engineer I architect the Android experience that brings independent journalism to millions of readers:

  • Insider streaming — architected live & VOD audio/video streaming on Media3/ExoPlayer, with Picture-in-Picture so the journalism follows you out of the app.
  • Podcasts — playback with offline downloads and pick-up-where-you-left-off resume across sessions.
  • Offline-first reading — the day's edition is there on the train, on the plane, or wherever the connection isn't.
  • Monetisation — AdMob integration woven into the reading experience without degrading it.
  • Architecture — an MVI multi-module structure with a reusable Jetpack Compose design system, keeping a growing feature set consistent and testable.

02 Insider — award-winning streaming

Insider is The Economist's video platform — live events and on-demand films inside the app. I played a key role in building it, and in June 2026 our team received the Professional Publishers Association's Video Content of the Year award for it.

🏆 PPA Award '26

Video Content of the Year — Professional Publishers Association, June 2026, for The Economist's Insider. Live streaming, VOD and Picture-in-Picture, built on Media3.

03 Architecture

The app runs on an MVI, multi-module architecture: unidirectional data flow keeps complex screens predictable, module boundaries keep build times and ownership clean, and a reusable Compose design system means every new feature ships looking and behaving like The Economist.
